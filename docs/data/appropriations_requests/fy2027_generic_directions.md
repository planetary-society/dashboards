## Benefits of Request

You will see a question like one of the following on the form:

* **Please describe the problem or issue your request will address.**  
* **Please describe the goals, results, and/or expected outcome of the request.**  
* **Please provide a justification for your request.**  
* **Please explain in a few sentences why any federal resources used to carry out this programmatic/language request would be an appropriate, constitutional use of taxpayer resources.**

Your response to any of these can be along the lines of the following. If you do not have a separate space to explain why this matters to your state/district, please include a few sentences about this in your response.

This request will allow NASA to remain on track to steadily advance existing and new decadal missions across all divisions, provide unique opportunities for the next generation of STEM professionals, and ensure that the U.S. can continue to be a global space science powerhouse. This request will allow the Astrophysics Division to push the boundaries of current detector technologies and better understand our early universe and habitable exoplanets through missions like the James Webb Space Telescope and the soon-to-be-launched Nancy Grace Roman Space Telescope. NASA’s Heliophysics research will enable deeper understanding of the space environment, thereby protecting existing terrestrial and space assets and helping to achieve new space exploration ambitions. Strong investment in the Planetary Science Division will help pursue several high-profile decadal missions, including the Lunar Discovery and Exploration Program and NEO Surveyor, the latter of which is a planetary defense mission set to monitor near-Earth asteroids.
