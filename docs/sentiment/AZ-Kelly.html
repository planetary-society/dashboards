About Mark Kelly

  • Earned a BSc in marine engineering and an MSc in aeronautical engineering.
  • Retired NASA astronaut; commanded two Space Shuttle missions, piloted two others.
  • Flew four space missions, including delivering modules to the International Space Station.
  • Served as a naval aviator and test pilot, flying 39 combat missions.
  • Co-founded aerospace company World View Enterprises; served on SpaceX safety panel.
