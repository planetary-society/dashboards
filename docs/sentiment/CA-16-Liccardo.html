Overall Summary

Representative Liccardo's direct influence on NASA's budget is limited by his lack of a seat on the House Science, Space, and Technology Committee. His public statements and legislative focus primarily address local economic issues like housing and cost of living, and while he champions innovation, his agenda broadly references "space" without specific emphasis on NASA's science mission directorates or Earth science. This suggests that advocating for increased NASA science funding, especially in light of a proposed 47% cut, may not immediately align with his most visible priorities.

However, there is significant potential for receptiveness due to the substantial economic impact of NASA science activities within his district.
