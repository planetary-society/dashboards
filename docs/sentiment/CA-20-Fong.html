  • Introduction of MACH Program: Representative Fong championed the Making Advancements in Commercial Hypersonics (MACH) Program, which authorizes NASA to support scientific experiments through high-speed flights, explicitly stating it helps NASA achieve its scientific goals and builds on the successful Flight Opportunities Program.
