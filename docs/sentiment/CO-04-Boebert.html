Opportunities

  • Advocacy for Colorado's Space Industry: Representative Boebert has consistently advocated for maintaining U.S. Space Command in Colorado, citing the state's significant aerospace economy and jobs, and has secured federal resources for NASA's Space Launch System, Orion Multi-Purpose Crew Vehicle, and Exploration Ground Systems. Colorado is home to the nation's second-largest aerospace economy, supporting over 232,000 jobs, with Pueblo (within her former district, now in CO-04) noted as benefiting from Space Command jobs.
