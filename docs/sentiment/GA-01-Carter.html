Overall Summary

Representative Carter's strong fiscal conservatism and consistent emphasis on reducing government spending present a significant challenge for advocating increased NASA space science funding. Furthermore, his expressed desire for stringent oversight of federally funded research suggests he may scrutinize NASA science budgets, particularly in light of proposed cuts.
