Overall Summary

Representative Beatty demonstrates a general pro-science and pro-research stance, evidenced by her support for STEM initiatives and active participation in the appropriations process for the subcommittee overseeing NASA's budget. Crucially, Ohio State University, her former institution, is a significant recipient of NASA science grants for exoplanet research and lidar technology, creating a direct local connection to increased NASA science funding. This local impact, coupled with her history of supporting research infrastructure through Community Project Funding, suggests a potential receptiveness to protecting and growing these investments.
