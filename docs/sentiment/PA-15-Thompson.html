Overall Summary

Representative Thompson's fiscal conservatism and skepticism regarding climate science present notable challenges for advocating increased NASA science funding, particularly for Earth science initiatives. His primary legislative focus on agriculture and education, combined with no direct space committee assignment, suggests that space science may not be a top-tier priority.

However, there are strong local connections that could foster receptiveness.
