About Representative David Schweikert (R-AZ-01)

  • Holds a Bachelor of Science in finance and real estate and an MBA from Arizona State University's W.P. Carey School of Business.
  • Began congressional service in 2011, representing Arizona's 5th, 6th, and 1st districts, following roles as Maricopa County treasurer and chair of the Arizona State Board of Equalization.
  • Proposes a debt reduction strategy centered on the 'radical adoption of technology,' specifically highlighting artificial intelligence as a key component.
  • Professional background includes working in the real estate industry and overseeing multi-billion dollar valuations and tax protests during his tenure on the Board of Equalization.
  • Voted against 2011 federal budget appropriations for the Smithsonian Institution, scientific research, and the development of high-speed rail.
