About Representative Juan Ciscomani (R-AZ-06)

  • Elected to represent Arizona's 6th congressional district in 2022 and re-elected in 2024.
  • Served as a senior adviser to former Arizona Governor Doug Ducey and as vice chair of the Arizona-Mexico Commission from 2015 to 2021.
  • Held professional roles at the University of Arizona as a program development specialist after becoming the first in his family to graduate from college.
  • Gained early legislative exposure through internships and fellowships with U.S. Representatives Ed Pastor and Loretta Sanchez.
  • Engaged with regional industry and commerce as a member and former vice president of outreach for the Tucson Hispanic Chamber of Commerce.
