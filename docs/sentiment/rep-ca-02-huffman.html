About Representative Jared Huffman (D-CA-02)

  • Served in the California State Assembly from 2006 to 2012 before being elected to the U.S. House of Representatives in 2012.
  • Worked as a senior attorney for the Natural Resources Defense Council and as a consumer attorney specializing in public interest and Title IX cases.
  • Co-founded the Congressional Freethought Caucus, which advocates for public policy formulated on the basis of reason and science.
  • Previously served on the board of the Marin Municipal Water District for 12 years, including three terms as president.
  • A former three-time All-American volleyball player and 1987 member of the USA Volleyball Team.
