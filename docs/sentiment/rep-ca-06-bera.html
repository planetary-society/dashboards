About Representative Ami Bera (D-CA-06)

  • A physician by profession, he earned a bachelor's degree in biological sciences and a Doctor of Medicine from the University of California, Irvine.
  • Served in several medical leadership roles prior to Congress, including chief medical officer for Sacramento County and medical director of Care Management at Mercy Healthcare for Sacramento.
  • Held academic positions in medicine as an associate dean for admissions and a clinical professor at the UC Davis School of Medicine.
  • Authored the Affordable Rabies Treatment for Uninsured Act following a personal encounter with a rabid fox on Capitol Hill, advocating for government reimbursement of treatment costs.
  • Has represented California in the U.S. House of Representatives since 2013 and previously served on the California State Board of Education.
