About Representative Sam T. Liccardo (D-CA-16)

  • Earned a bachelor's degree in government from Georgetown University, followed by a Juris Doctor and Master of Public Policy from Harvard Law School and Harvard Kennedy School.
  • Served as a criminal prosecutor in the Santa Clara County District Attorney's office before being elected to the San Jose City Council and later serving two terms as the Mayor of San Jose.
  • Launched the 'Smart City Vision' as mayor, aiming to make San Jose a leader in innovation through partnerships with technology companies like Facebook and Mastercard.
  • Spearheaded digital inclusion initiatives such as the San Jose Digital Inclusion Fund to expand broadband access and distribution of hotspots to students and low-income residents.
  • Advocated for significant regional transportation and energy projects, including the expansion of BART and Caltrain, and the launch of the San Jose Clean Energy community choice program.
