About Representative George Whitesides (D-CA-27)

  • Served as Chief of Staff at NASA under Administrator Charles Bolden and received the NASA Distinguished Service Medal.
  • Led Virgin Galactic as its first CEO for a decade, overseeing the development of commercial spaceflight vehicles and operations at Mojave Air and Space Port.
  • Chaired the Reusable Launch Vehicle Working Group for the FAA's Commercial Space Transportation advisory committee (COMSTAC).
  • Holds an MPhil in geographic information systems and remote sensing from King's College, Cambridge, and was a Fulbright Scholar.
  • Co-founded a wildfire prevention organization and co-sponsored the bipartisan Fix Our Forests Act and the DOE and NASA Interagency Research Coordination Act.
