About Representative Laura Friedman (D-CA-30)

  • Served as a member of the California State Assembly from 2016 to 2024 representing the 44th district.
  • Held local office as a member of the Glendale City Council from 2009 to 2016, including a term as mayor from 2011 to 2012.
  • Possesses extensive private sector experience in the entertainment industry, having served as a film producer and vice president of development at Rysher Entertainment and Cort/Madden Company.
  • Authored state legislation focused on infrastructure and transportation, including a law eliminating parking requirements near mass transit and oversight of California High-Speed Rail bonds.
  • Earned a Bachelor of Arts degree from the University of Rochester and has owned a web-based art glass dealership since 2000.
