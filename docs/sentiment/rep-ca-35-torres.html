About Representative Norma J. Torres (D-CA-35)

  • Born in Guatemala, she immigrated to the United States at age five and gained citizenship in 1992.
  • Worked professionally as a 9-1-1 dispatcher and led a 1994 campaign to require the hiring of bilingual operators.
  • Earned a bachelor's degree in labor studies from the National Labor College in 2012.
  • Served in local and state government as a Pomona city council member, mayor, and member of the California State Assembly and State Senate.
  • Elected to the U.S. House of Representatives for California's 35th congressional district in 2014 and has served consecutive terms since.
