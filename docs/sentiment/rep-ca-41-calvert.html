You should frame Science Mission Directorate funding as the essential foundation for the California aerospace workforce and the national treasures like JPL that he has historically vowed to protect.

Bridge issues:

About Representative Ken Calvert (R-CA-41)

  • Served as a small business owner in the restaurant and real estate industries prior to his election to Congress.
  • Chaired the Space and Aeronautics Subcommittee during the 109th Congress, overseeing NASA and authoring the NASA Authorization Act of 2005.
  • Holds an Associate of Arts degree in business from Chaffey Community College and a Bachelor of Arts degree from San Diego State University.
  • Authored the E-Verify law, an employment verification program originally established as the Basic Pilot Program in 1995.
  • Advocates for strategic military reviews that prioritize modern warfare capabilities over traditional assets like aircraft carriers and Abrams tanks.
