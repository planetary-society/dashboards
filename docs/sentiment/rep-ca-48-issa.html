About Representative Darrell Issa (R-CA-48)

  • Served in the U.S. Army as an Explosive Ordnance Disposal (EOD) technician and later as a captain in the Army Reserve, receiving a commendation for performance that exceeded contemporaries.
  • Co-founded Directed Electronics in 1982, serving as CEO of the company which became a major manufacturer of automotive security and convenience products.
  • Holds a degree in business administration from Siena Heights University and completed additional studies at Kent State University, where he was commissioned as a second lieutenant through ROTC.
  • Introduced and sponsored several technology-related bills, including the Federal Information Technology Acquisition Reform Act and the Federal Information Security Modernization Act of 2014.
  • Expressed support for embryonic stem cell research and has consistently sponsored legislation regarding research works and intellectual property rights for artists and musicians.
