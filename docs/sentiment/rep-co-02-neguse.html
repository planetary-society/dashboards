About Representative Joe Neguse (D-CO-02)

  • Served as a regent of the University of Colorado from 2008 to 2015 and holds a bachelor's degree in political science and economics from the University of Colorado Boulder.
  • Appointed as the executive director of the Colorado Department of Regulatory Agencies (DORA) in 2015, overseeing state regulatory functions.
  • Maintains a legislative focus on climate change, characterizing it as an existential threat and sponsoring legislation for forest management and wildfire mitigation.
  • Supports federal incentives for renewable energy use and increased fuel efficiency standards.
  • Advocates for environmental protections, including sponsoring bills for wildlife protections on the South Platte River and expanding the Arapaho National Forest.
