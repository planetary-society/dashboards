Lead with: The Protector of Research Stability

Lead by thanking the Congresswoman for her direct legislative leadership in shielding NASA's Science Mission Directorate (SMD) from budget instability during continuing resolutions. You should frame NASA space science not just as an exploration goal, but as a critical component of the federal research ecosystem that she has fought to protect from funding 'trickles.'

Bridge issues:

  • Federal Research Stability: "Just as you've spoken out against NIH research grants dwindling to a trickle, the $5.4 million in NASA science funding flowing to Yale and local businesses in our district is currently at risk of the same damaging slowdowns."

About Representative Rosa L. DeLauro (D-CT-03)

  • Earned a bachelor's degree from Marymount College, attended the London School of Economics, and holds a master's degree in international politics from Columbia University.
  • Served as chief of staff and campaign manager for Senator Chris Dodd and as executive director of EMILY's List prior to her 1991 election to Congress.
  • Focuses on health policy and biomedical research, authoring legislation for cancer research and the FDA Food Safety Modernization Act.
  • Advocates for alternative protein science and federal research funding parity for cultivated foods to maintain U.S. global leadership in the field.
  • Criticized the food industry and animal farming practices, proposing the creation of a separate Food Safety Administration to oversee public health concerns.
