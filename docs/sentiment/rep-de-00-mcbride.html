About Representative Sarah McBride (D-DE At-Large)

  • Elected as the first openly transgender member of the United States Congress in 2024, representing Delaware's at-large district.
  • Served in the Delaware Senate from 2021 to 2025, where she sponsored the Healthy Delaware Families Act to establish paid family and medical leave.
  • Held the position of national press secretary for the Human Rights Campaign from 2016 to 2021 and interned at the White House Office of Public Engagement and Intergovernmental Affairs in 2012.
  • Earned a bachelor's degree from American University in 2013, where she served as student government president and worked on advocacy regarding conflict-free minerals and academic regulations.
  • Advocated for Delaware state legislation banning discrimination based on gender identity in employment, housing, insurance, and public accommodations.
