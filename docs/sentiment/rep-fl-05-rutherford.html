About Representative John H. Rutherford (R-FL-05)

  • Served in law enforcement for 41 years with the Jacksonville Sheriff's Office, including 12 years as the elected Duval County Sheriff.
  • Earned an Associate of Science in criminology from Florida State College at Jacksonville and a Bachelor of Science in criminology from Florida State University.
  • Advocates for military interests related to Naval Air Station Jacksonville and Naval Station Mayport, including the basing of F-35 Lightning II squadrons.
  • Maintains a skeptical posture toward the scientific consensus on human-caused climate change while expressing concern over sea level rise in his district.
  • Introduced the Atlantic Coastal Economies Protection Act to prohibit seismic air gun testing and has supported moratoriums on offshore oil and gas drilling.
