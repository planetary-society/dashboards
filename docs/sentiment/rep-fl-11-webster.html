About Representative Daniel Webster (R-FL-11)

  • Earned a Bachelor of Science degree in electrical engineering from the Georgia Institute of Technology in 1971.
  • Owns and operates a family air conditioning and heating business, which he joined following his college graduation.
  • Served 28 years in the Florida legislature, including tenures as the first Republican Speaker of the Florida House since Reconstruction and as Florida Senate Majority Leader.
  • Became eligible for the U.S. military draft after college but was not conscripted due to failing a physical exam for lifelong foot problems.
  • Signed a pledge to oppose climate change legislation that would result in a net increase in government revenue.
