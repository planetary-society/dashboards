About Representative Sheila Cherfilus-McCormick (D-FL-20)

  • Served as a project manager for the New York City Transit Authority following her graduation from Howard University.
  • Held executive leadership roles, including Vice President for Operations and CEO, at Trinity Health Care Services, a family-owned home health care company.
  • Earned a Bachelor of Arts in political science and government from Howard University and a Juris Doctor from St. Thomas University School of Law.
  • Elected as the first Haitian-American Democrat to the U.S. House of Representatives, representing Florida's 20th district since 2022.
  • Faced a 2025 federal indictment and a 2026 House Ethics Committee finding regarding the alleged theft of $5 million in FEMA funds and campaign finance violations.
