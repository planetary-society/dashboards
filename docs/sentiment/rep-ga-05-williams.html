About Representative Nikema Williams (D-GA-05)

  • Earned a Bachelor of Arts degree in biology from Talladega College.
  • Served as a member of the Georgia State Senate for the 39th district from 2017 to 2021.
  • Held leadership roles in advocacy organizations, including serving as vice president for public policy at Planned Parenthood Southeast and State Director of the National Domestic Workers Alliance.
  • Served as Chair of the Democratic Party of Georgia from 2019 to 2025 and was a member of the Electoral College in 2020.
  • Elected to the U.S. House of Representatives in 2020 to represent Georgia's 5th congressional district, succeeding John Lewis.
