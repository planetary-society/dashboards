About Representative Ashley Hinson (R-IA-02)

  • Served in the Iowa House of Representatives from 2017 to 2021, where she chaired the Transportation Committee and served on the Transportation, Infrastructure, and Capitals Appropriations Subcommittee.
  • Holds a degree in broadcast journalism from the University of Southern California and worked professionally as a television news anchor and reporter for KCRG-TV.
  • Previous experience in media production includes working as a camera operator and editor for evening newscasts at WOI-TV in Des Moines.
  • Has served in the U.S. House of Representatives since 2021, representing Iowa's 1st and 2nd congressional districts.
  • Introduced the Ending Agricultural Trade Suppression (EATS) Act in 2023 to prohibit state and local governments from imposing production standards on agricultural goods in interstate commerce.
