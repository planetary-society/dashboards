About Representative Mike Quigley (D-IL-05)

  • Holds a Juris Doctor from Loyola University Chicago School of Law and a Master’s degree in public policy from the University of Chicago.
  • Served as an adjunct professor at Loyola University Chicago, where he taught environmental policy and Chicago politics.
