About Representative Morgan McGarvey (D-KY-03)

  • Elected to the U.S. House of Representatives in 2022, representing Kentucky's 3rd congressional district.
  • Served in the Kentucky Senate from 2012 to 2023, including a tenure as minority floor leader starting in 2018.
  • Holds a Juris Doctor from the University of Kentucky College of Law and a bachelor's degree in journalism from the University of Missouri.
  • Practiced law at Morgan Pottinger McGarvey and served as a Special Assistant Attorney General of Kentucky.
  • Previously worked for U.S. Representative Ben Chandler and the law firm Frost Brown Todd.
