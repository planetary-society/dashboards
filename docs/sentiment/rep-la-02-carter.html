Meeting Strategy

Lead with: NASA Science as the Innovation Engine for New Orleans

Lead with his deep pride in the Michoud Assembly Facility and the local workforce that builds the Space Launch System. You should frame NASA Science Mission Directorate funding as the essential driver of the research and discovery that ensures a long-term, diverse workload for the district's aerospace sector.

Bridge issues:

  • Education and STEM Workforce: "You’ve been a champion for local students through STEM NOLA, and robust funding for NASA’s science missions provides the actual discoveries that inspire these students to become the next generation of New Orleans aerospace leaders."
  • Local Economic Advancement: "Beyond heavy manufacturing, NASA science is active in our district today through the air quality monitoring research at Xavier University, showing how NASA investment supports both our local economy and public health."

Watch out for:

  • Avoid letting the conversation pivot exclusively to the Space Launch System or Artemis; while he is a major supporter of these, you must steer the focus back to how science missions utilize that infrastructure and provide the 'why' behind the 'how'.
  • While the Representative recently signed the FY27 science support letter, avoid assuming he has a deep history with specific planetary or astrophysics missions, as his public record is much more focused on exploration and local jobs.

Expect this reaction: The Representative is likely to be very supportive, especially if science is framed as a protector of local jobs and a tool for STEM inspiration. His recent signature on the FY27 science budget letter indicates he is ready to be a direct ally on funding.

  • Competing Priority: His primary focus on the Space Launch System (SLS) and Artemis program may lead him to prioritize human exploration funding over science mission accounts.2 7

  • Consider mentioning his role on the Energy and Commerce Environment subcommittee to highlight how NASA Earth Science data—like the work at Xavier University—is a vital tool for monitoring local air quality and public health.
