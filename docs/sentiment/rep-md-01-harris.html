About Representative Andy Harris (R-MD-01)

  • Served in the Navy Medical Corps and the U.S. Naval Reserve as a commander on active duty during Operation Desert Storm.
  • Earned a BS in biology and an MD from Johns Hopkins University, followed by an MHS in health policy and management.
  • Worked as an anesthesiologist, associate professor of anesthesiology and critical care medicine, and chief of obstetric anesthesiology at Johns Hopkins Hospital.
  • Maintained a medical practice while serving as a 'citizen-legislator' in the Maryland State Senate prior to his congressional service.
  • Supports technological developments such as hydrogen cells and nuclear fusion to address climate change and has played a role in funding Chesapeake Bay cleanup efforts.
