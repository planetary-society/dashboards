Bridge issues:

About Representative Sarah Elfreth (D-MD-03)

  • Earned a Bachelor of Arts in political science from Towson University and a Master of Public Policy from Johns Hopkins University.
  • Served as a student member of the University System of Maryland Board of Regents and worked as a research assistant at Johns Hopkins University.
  • Held professional roles as a lobbyist for the National Aquarium and Johns Hopkins University, and as a senior director for the consulting firm Margrave Strategies.
  • Served in the Maryland Senate from 2019 to 2025, where she chaired the Chesapeake Executive Council and the Joint Committee on the Chesapeake and Atlantic Coastal Bay Critical Areas.
  • Passed the David Perez Military Heroes Act to provide state funding for psychedelic research to assist military veterans with post-traumatic stress disorder.
