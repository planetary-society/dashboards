Lead with: Lead with Protecting Maryland’s 'Scientific Crown Jewels'

You should emphasize that cutting the Science Mission Directorate budget directly threatens the Maryland-based jobs and innovation he has fought fiercely to defend.

Bridge issues:

  • Protection of Regional Federal Investments: "Supporting a robust $9 billion budget for NASA science ensures that Maryland institutions like Goddard and APL remain the global leaders in exploring our universe, protecting thousands of local jobs and nearly a billion dollars in regional economic activity."

Expect this reaction: You will likely find an enthusiastic champion in this office who is already energized to fight budget cuts. The staff will likely be receptive and may ask for specific data on how national budget proposals impact the mission portfolio at Goddard Space Flight Center.

About Representative Kweisi Mfume (D-MD-07)

  • Earned a Bachelor of Science degree from Morgan State University in 1976 and a Master of Science degree from Johns Hopkins University in 1984.
  • Served in the U.S. House of Representatives from 1987 to 1996 and returned to the seat in 2020.
  • Held the position of chief executive officer of the National Medical Association (NMA) from 2010 to 2011.
  • Served as the principal investigator for the Health Policy Research Consortium from 2013 to 2018.
  • Appointed chair of the board of regents for Morgan State University in 2013.
