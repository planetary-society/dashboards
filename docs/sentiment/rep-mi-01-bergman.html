About Representative Jack Bergman (R-MI-01)

  • Retired United States Marine Corps lieutenant general who served as commanding general of the Marine Forces Reserve and Marine Forces North.
  • Extensive background as a naval aviator, flying both rotary-winged aircraft (CH-46, UH-1) and fixed-wing aircraft (T-28, KC-130).
  • Holds a Bachelor of Arts in business from Gustavus Adolphus College and a Master of Business Administration from the University of West Florida.
  • Completed advanced military technical education including Naval Aviation Flight Training, LOGTECH, and the Syracuse University National Security Seminar.
  • Co-led a bipartisan effort to secure the designation of the National All-Domain Warfighting Center as a national range for uncrewed aerial systems testing.
