About Representative Tim Walberg (R-MI-05)

  • Serves as the U.S. representative for Michigan's 5th congressional district, having previously represented the 7th district from 2007 to 2009 and 2011 to 2023.
  • Briefly served with the U.S. Forest Service early in his career following high school graduation in 1969.
  • Served 16 years as a member of the Michigan House of Representatives from 1983 to 1998.
  • Formally rejects the scientific consensus on climate change, stating his belief that a creator will address environmental problems.
  • Worked professionally as a pastor for Grace Fellowship Church and as a division manager for the Moody Bible Institute in Chicago.
