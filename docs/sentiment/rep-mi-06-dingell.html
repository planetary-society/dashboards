About Representative Debbie Dingell (D-MI-06)

  • Served as president of the General Motors Foundation and as executive director of global community relations and government relations at General Motors.
  • Worked as a consultant to the American Automobile Policy Council.
  • Founder and past chair of the National Women's Health Resource Center and the Children's Inn at the National Institutes of Health (NIH).
  • Earned a degree from the Edmund A. Walsh School of Foreign Service at Georgetown University in 1975.
  • Served on the board of governors for Wayne State University and the board of directors for Vital Voices Global Partnership.
