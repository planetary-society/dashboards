Meeting Strategy

Lead with: The Appropriations Architect for Science and Security

Lead by recognizing her senior leadership on the Appropriations Committee and her past success in securing funding increases for NASA's Earth science and climate research. You should position the entire Science Mission Directorate as the essential engine that drives the STEM workforce and technological superiority she prioritizes for national security.

Bridge issues:

  • Environment & Earth Science: "You have been a champion for NASA’s efforts to study our changing climate, and we urge you to protect the Science Mission Directorate budget so NASA can continue providing the vital Earth science data you have consistently supported in past funding bills."
  • STEM Workforce & National Superiority: "NASA’s science missions are the ultimate training ground for the physicists and engineers you’ve identified as critical to our nation's technological superiority in the air and space domains."

Watch out for:

  • Avoid assuming she is already a dedicated advocate for planetary exploration or deep-space missions, as her public record is almost entirely focused on Earth science and defense-related space issues.
  • Caution against characterizing her as an automatic 'yes' on specific Science Mission Directorate funding levels; while she supports NASA broadly, she has not signed recent letters requesting the $9 billion science topline.

Expect this reaction: The office will likely be receptive and polite, but given her focus on Defense appropriations and recent budget constraints, the reaction may be conditional on how NASA science fits into broader fiscal priorities.

About Representative Betty McCollum (D-MN-04)

  • Serves as the U.S. Representative for Minnesota's 4th congressional district since 2001 and is the dean of Minnesota's congressional delegation.
  • Earned an Associate of Arts from Inver Hills Community College and a bachelor's degree in social studies and education from the College of St. Catherine.
  • Professional background includes experience as a high school social studies teacher and a retail store manager for Dayton's and Sears.
  • Served eight years as a state representative in the Minnesota House of Representatives and two years on the North St. Paul city council.
  • Co-founded the Congressional Global Health Caucus and the Quality Care Coalition, and co-chairs the United States Congressional International Conservation Caucus.
