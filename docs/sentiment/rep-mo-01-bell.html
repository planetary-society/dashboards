About Representative Wesley Bell (D-MO-01)

  • Serves as the U.S. Representative for Missouri's 1st congressional district, having previously served as the Prosecuting Attorney for St. Louis County from 2019 to 2025.
  • Held academic and judicial roles prior to federal service, including serving as a professor in the criminology department at Florissant Valley Community College and as a municipal court judge in Velda City.
  • Elected to the Ferguson City Council in 2015 following the Ferguson protests, later running for county prosecutor on a platform of community-based policing and criminal justice reform.
  • Maintains a background in law and public service as a former St. Louis County public defender and municipal prosecutor in Riverview.
  • Holds a Juris Doctor from the University of Missouri School of Law and an undergraduate degree from Lindenwood University.
