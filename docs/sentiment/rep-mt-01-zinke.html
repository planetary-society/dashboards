About Representative Ryan K. Zinke (R-MT At-Large)

  • Served as a U.S. Navy SEAL from 1986 to 2008, retiring at the rank of commander after leading special operations personnel in Iraq and establishing the Naval Special Warfare Advanced Training Command.
  • Earned a Bachelor of Science degree in geology from the University of Oregon in 1984, as well as a Master of Business Administration and a Master of Science in global leadership.
  • Served as the United States Secretary of the Interior from 2017 to 2019, where he opened federal lands for oil, gas, and mineral exploration and extraction.
  • Held private sector roles as a board member for the oil pipeline company QS Energy and as managing director for the cryptocurrency investment firm Artillery One.
  • Represented Montana in the U.S. House of Representatives from 2015 to 2017 and again starting in 2023, following service in the Montana Senate from 2009 to 2013.
