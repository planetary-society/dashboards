About Representative LaMonica McIver (D-NJ-10)

  • Served on the Newark Municipal Council from 2018 to 2024, including a term as council president starting in 2022.
  • Holds a master’s degree in educational leadership and policy from Seton Hall University and a degree in English Literature from Bloomfield College.
  • Worked as a public affairs manager for Newark's Public Service Enterprise Group (PSEG) and as a personnel director for Montclair Public Schools.
  • Co-founded Newark G.A.L.S., a non-profit organization focused on fostering leadership skills in young women and girls.
  • Elected to the U.S. House of Representatives in a 2024 special election to succeed Donald Payne Jr.
