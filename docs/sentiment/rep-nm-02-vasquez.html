About Representative Gabe Vasquez (D-NM-02)

  • Elected to the U.S. House of Representatives in 2022, representing New Mexico's 2nd district after serving on the Las Cruces City Council from 2017 to 2021.
  • Served as a field representative for U.S. Senator Martin Heinrich and held leadership roles at the New Mexico Wildlife Federation and the Wilderness Society.
  • Earned a Bachelor of Arts in English and journalism from New Mexico State University, where he served as editor-in-chief of the student newspaper.
  • Professional background includes serving as executive director of the Las Cruces Hispanic Chamber of Commerce and executive vice president for public relations at SDS Inc.
  • Exposed to technical trades in childhood through his grandfather's television repair shop in Ciudad Juárez, Mexico.
