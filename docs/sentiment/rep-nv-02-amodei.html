About Representative Mark E. Amodei (R-NV-02)

  • Served in the United States Army Judge Advocate General (JAG) Corps from 1983 to 1987 as a prosecutor and Assistant U.S. Attorney.
  • Earned a Bachelor of Arts in political science from the University of Nevada and a Juris Doctor from the University of the Pacific McGeorge School of Law.
  • Served as the president of the Nevada Mining Association from 2007 to 2008.
  • Held leadership roles in the Nevada Legislature, including serving as president pro tempore of the Nevada Senate and as a member of the Nevada Assembly.
  • Introduced the Northern Nevada Land Conservation and Economic Development Act and other legislation focused on federal land conveyance and management.
