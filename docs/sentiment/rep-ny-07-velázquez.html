Lead with: Science as a Local Economic and Innovation Engine

Lead with the immense impact of NASA’s planetary science contracts in Brooklyn, specifically through Honeybee Robotics. You should frame NASA Science Mission Directorate (SMD) funding not just as a research goal, but as a primary driver of high-skilled jobs and small business innovation within her district.

Bridge issues:

  • Small Business and Innovation: "As a champion of the SBIR and STTR programs, you can see how NASA science relies on these exact pipelines to develop the drilling and robotics technology currently being built right here in Brooklyn for missions to Mars and Europa."

About Representative Nydia M. Velázquez (D-NY-07)

  • First Puerto Rican woman to serve in the U.S. Congress, representing New York since 1993.
  • Earned a B.A. and M.A. in political science and served as a university instructor and adjunct professor of Puerto Rican studies.
  • Led the Vieques movement to end U.S. military bomb testing on the island, resulting in the closure of the Atlantic Fleet Weapons Training Facility and Roosevelt Roads Naval Station.
  • Pioneered the 'Atrévete Con Tu Voto' program to empower Latino voters through registration and political engagement.
  • Served as the first Hispanic woman on the New York City Council and the first Hispanic woman to chair a House standing committee.
