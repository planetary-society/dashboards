About Representative Daniel S. Goldman (D-NY-10)

  • Served as an assistant United States attorney in the Southern District of New York from 2007 to 2017, prosecuting organized crime, securities fraud, and white-collar crime.
  • Held the roles of Senior Advisor and Director of Investigations for the House Intelligence Committee and lead counsel for the first impeachment inquiry against Donald Trump.
  • Earned a Bachelor of Arts in history from Yale University and a Juris Doctor with distinction from Stanford Law School.
  • Disclosed personal investments in defense contractors Lockheed Martin and Northrop Grumman, as well as energy companies Chevron, ExxonMobil, and Halliburton.
  • Worked as an Olympics researcher and writer for NBC Sports prior to attending law school.
