About Representative Joseph D. Morelle (D-NY-25)

  • Earned a Bachelor of Arts degree in political science from the State University of New York at Geneseo in 1986.
  • Authored a 2005 report titled "Creating a State of Innovation: Unleashing The Power of New York's Entrepreneurial Economy" which offered policy recommendations for the state's economy.
