Bottom Line

Champion (high confidence): Representative Kaptur is a veteran advocate for NASA with deep institutional influence as a senior member of the Appropriations Committee. She recently took a firm, public stand against proposed cuts to the Science Mission Directorate, emphasizing the need to protect the workforce and ongoing programs. Advocates should frame NASA science as a vital engine for Ohio's high-tech manufacturing and academic sectors, which she has spent decades defending.

Meeting Strategy

Lead with: The Senior Appropriator and NASA Champion

Lead with gratitude for her recent leadership in signing the July 2025 letter that firmly rejected proposed cuts to the Science Mission Directorate. Frame your request as a way to support her ongoing efforts to protect the high-tech workforce and world-class research occurring at the University of Toledo and the Neil Armstrong Test Facility.

Bridge issues:

  • Jobs and the Economy: "NASA science is a direct engine for Northwest Ohio's economy, supporting hundreds of millions in local contracts for companies like Sierra Lobo that provide essential environmental testing for our space missions."
  • Energy and the Environment: "Programs like 'Mission Earth' at the University of Toledo demonstrate how NASA science provides the critical data we need to protect the Great Lakes and drive innovation in environmental research."

Watch out for:

  • Be mindful that as a senior Appropriator, she must balance NASA science against other critical research budgets like the DOE Office of Science; emphasize that NASA's space science missions provide unique data that cannot be replicated elsewhere.
  • Avoid getting lost in the technical specifics of heliophysics or biological sciences where her record is less defined; instead, focus on the broad Science Mission Directorate topline she has already publicly defended.

Expect this reaction: You should expect a very warm and knowledgeable reception. As a co-founder of the NASA Caucus and a recent signatory on SMD-specific defense letters, she and her staff are likely to be among the most receptive in Congress to your message.

Key Challenges

  • Competing Priority: As a senior Appropriator, she faces intense pressure to balance NASA funding against other science priorities, such as the DOE Office of Science.8

  • Consider mentioning her successful legislative effort to rename the Neil A. Armstrong Test Facility to highlight her deep personal and historical commitment to NASA's presence in Ohio.

About Representative Marcy Kaptur (D-OH-09)

  • Earned a Master of Urban Planning from the University of Michigan and began doctoral studies in urban planning development finance at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology.
  • Served as a professional urban planner for the Toledo-Lucas County Plan Commissions and as a domestic policy advisor on urban affairs during the Carter administration.
  • Advocated for patent reform by opposing the America Invents Act, arguing that shifting to a 'first to file' framework disadvantaged small businesses and inventors.
  • Authored the legislation that established the National World War II Memorial and served as a vocal critic of the 2008 financial crisis and subsequent bank bailouts.
  • Holds the record as the longest-serving woman in the history of the United States Congress, having served as a representative for Ohio since 1983.
