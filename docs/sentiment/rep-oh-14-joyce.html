Meeting Strategy

Lead with: Leverage Appropriations Influence and Local Aerospace Leadership

Lead with the Representative's influential role on the Appropriations Committee and his established record of defending NASA's footprint in Ohio. Frame NASA science as the essential research engine that sustains the high-tech workforce at NASA Glenn and the academic excellence of local institutions like Kent State. Given the thin direct SMD record, treat this as the best available opening rather than evidence of likely support.

Bridge issues:

  • Great Lakes Conservation: "As a leader in the Great Lakes Restoration Initiative, you understand the need for high-quality data; NASA Earth science missions provide the unique space-based monitoring necessary to protect the environmental and economic health of our region's waters."
  • STEM Education and Workforce: "Your commitment to STEM, seen through the Congressional App Challenge, is directly fueled by NASA's science missions, which inspire students in the 14th District to pursue the technical degrees required for Ohio's future aerospace workforce."

Watch out for:

  • Avoid presenting the Representative as a committed supporter of the Science Mission Directorate specifically, as he has not signed onto recent funding letters despite his broad support for NASA.
  • Do not oversell science-specific support when the public record is thin; keep the ask anchored in local value and broad NASA space science.

Expect this reaction: The Representative's staff is likely to be politely receptive to arguments about local economic impact and STEM, but may remain non-committal on specific funding increases for science divisions due to his focus on fiscal responsibility.

About Representative David P. Joyce (R-OH-14)

  • Holds a Bachelor of Science degree in accounting from the University of Dayton and a Juris Doctor.
  • Served as a public defender and county prosecutor in Ohio, including a tenure as the prosecutor of Geauga County.
  • Has served in the United States House of Representatives for Ohio's 14th congressional district since 2013.
  • Advocates for cybersecurity infrastructure enhancements and providing resources for the intelligence community and military.
