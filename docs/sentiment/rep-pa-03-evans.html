Bridge issues:

About Representative Dwight Evans (D-PA-03)

  • Serves as the U.S. representative for Pennsylvania's 3rd congressional district, having first entered Congress via a special election in 2016.
  • Served in the Pennsylvania House of Representatives for 35 years from 1981 to 2016, including 20 years as the Democratic chairman of the House Appropriations Committee.
  • Began his career as a teacher in the School District of Philadelphia and worked as an employment counselor for the Urban League.
  • Earned degrees from the Community College of Philadelphia (1973) and La Salle University (1975).
  • Announced in June 2025 that he will not seek reelection in 2026, intending to serve the remainder of his current term.
