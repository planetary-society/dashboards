About Representative Madeleine Dean (D-PA-04)

  • Served as a Democratic member of the Pennsylvania General Assembly representing the 153rd district from 2012 until her election to Congress in 2018.
  • Practiced law as a trial lawyer, served as executive director for the Philadelphia Trial Lawyers, and operated a small private law practice.
  • Spent 10 years as an assistant professor of English at La Salle University, where she taught writing and ethics.
  • Held local government roles as an Abington Township committeewoman and Abington Township commissioner.
  • Maintains industry ties to the bicycle business through her husband, an entrepreneur who managed Advanced Sports International.
