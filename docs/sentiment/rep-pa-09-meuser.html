About Representative Daniel Meuser (R-PA-09)

  • Served as the Secretary of Revenue for the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania under Governor Tom Corbett, where he oversaw state tax law enforcement and budget projections.
  • Spent over two decades as an executive and president at Pride Mobility Products, a manufacturer of motorized wheelchairs, helping grow the company from $2 million to over $400 million in sales.
  • Sponsored the American Tech Workforce Act of 2021, which sought to establish a wage floor for the high-skill H-1B visa program and eliminate the Optional Practical Training program for foreign graduates.
  • Testified before Congress regarding federal practices and rights for the disabled, and received HomeCare magazine's HomeCaring Award for his work in the medical equipment industry.
  • Holds a background in private sector management and currently serves as a board member and consultant for the Pride Corporation.
