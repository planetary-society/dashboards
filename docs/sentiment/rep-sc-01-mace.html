About Representative Nancy Mace (R-SC-01)

  • In 1999, she became the first woman to graduate from the Corps of Cadets program at the Citadel Military College of South Carolina.
  • Founded the Mace Group, a public relations and consulting firm, in 2008 and previously co-owned the political news website FITSNews.
  • Served in the South Carolina House of Representatives from 2018 to 2020 before being elected to the U.S. House of Representatives in 2020.
  • Introduced the MACE Act to modernize federal cybersecurity job requirements and the PAAW Act to limit federal funding for animal testing.
  • Chaired congressional hearings on Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena (UAPs) and government transparency as a subcommittee chairwoman.
