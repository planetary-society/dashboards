About Representative Charles J. "Chuck" Fleischmann (R-TN-03)

  • Has served as the U.S. Representative for Tennessee's 3rd congressional district since 2011, representing a district that includes Chattanooga and Oak Ridge.
  • Earned a Bachelor of Arts in political science from the University of Illinois at Urbana–Champaign and a Juris Doctor from the University of Tennessee College of Law.
  • Founded an independent law firm in 1987 and served as president of the Chattanooga Bar Association prior to his congressional service.
  • Advocates for the use of nuclear power, serves as the head of the House Nuclear Cleanup Caucus, and has proposed Department of Energy funding for advanced nuclear energy projects.
  • Introduced the Stop Green Initiative Abuse Act of 2011 to repeal the Department of Energy's Weatherization Assistance Program.
