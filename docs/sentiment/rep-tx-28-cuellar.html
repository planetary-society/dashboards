About Representative Henry Cuellar (D-TX-28)

  • Holds a Bachelor of Science in foreign service from Georgetown University, a Master of Arts in international trade, a Juris Doctor, and a Ph.D. from the University of Texas at Austin.
  • Completing a master's degree in defense and strategic studies from the Naval War College as of 2025.
  • Served 14 years in the Texas House of Representatives and held the statewide office of Texas Secretary of State in 2001.
  • Professional background includes operating a law firm, serving as a licensed customs broker, and teaching as an adjunct professor of international commercial law.
  • Advocated for international infrastructure projects, including the expansion of passenger rail between San Antonio, Texas, and Monterrey, Mexico.
