About Representative Celeste Maloy (R-UT-02)

  • Earned a degree in agriculture from Southern Utah University and a Juris Doctor from Brigham Young University.
  • Worked for 11 years as a soil conservationist for the Natural Resources Conservation Service in Utah.
  • Served as deputy county attorney for Washington County, Utah, specializing in public land law and water policy.
  • Advocated for Utah landowners before the U.S. House Natural Resources Subcommittee on Oversight and Investigations regarding land rights.
