About Representative Mike Kennedy (R-UT-03)

  • Earned a Bachelor of Science from Brigham Young University and a Doctor of Medicine from Michigan State University.
  • Holds a Juris Doctor from Brigham Young University's J. Reuben Clark Law School, which he completed while managing a medical practice.
  • Works as a family doctor for Premier Family Medical Group in Alpine, Utah.
  • Served in the Utah House of Representatives from 2013 to 2019 and the Utah Senate from 2021 to 2025.
  • Began service as the U.S. Representative for Utah's 3rd congressional district in January 2025.
