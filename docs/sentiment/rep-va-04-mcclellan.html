About Representative Jennifer L. McClellan (D-VA-04)

  • Elected as the first Black woman to represent Virginia in Congress after winning a special election in 2023 for the 4th district.
  • Served in the Virginia General Assembly from 2006 to 2023, including roles in the House of Delegates and the Virginia State Senate.
  • Held professional industry experience as regulatory counsel for Verizon Communications and practiced law at Hunton & Williams.
  • Sponsored state-level environmental legislation including the Virginia Clean Economy Act.
  • Earned a Bachelor of Arts in English and political science from the University of Richmond and a Juris Doctor from the University of Virginia School of Law.
