About Representative Ben Cline (R-VA-06)

  • Served as president of NDS Corporation from 2002 to 2007, a company providing sales and marketing assistance to rural internet and technology businesses.
  • Gained extensive legislative experience as a member of the Virginia House of Delegates from 2002 to 2018, where he chaired the Militia, Police and Public Safety Committee.
  • Held a senior staff role in the U.S. House of Representatives from 1994 to 2002, rising from legislative correspondent to chief of staff for Representative Bob Goodlatte.
  • Practiced law as an assistant Commonwealth's Attorney for Rockingham County and the city of Harrisonburg, and later maintained a private law practice.
  • Earned a B.A. from Bates College in 1994 and a J.D. from the University of Richmond School of Law in 2007.
