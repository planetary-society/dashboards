About Representative H. Morgan Griffith (R-VA-09)

  • Served in the Virginia House of Delegates from 1994 to 2011, including a tenure as House Majority Leader.
  • Earned a Bachelor of Arts from Emory and Henry College and a J.D. from the Washington and Lee University School of Law.
  • Worked as an attorney in private practice and led the Roanoke/Salem office of the law firm Albo & Oblon LLP.
  • Elected to the U.S. House of Representatives in 2010, representing Virginia's 9th congressional district since 2011.
  • Has expressed skepticism regarding anthropogenic climate change, citing historical warming and observations of Martian ice caps.
