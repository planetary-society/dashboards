Bridge issues:

  • STEM Workforce Development: "NASA grants to Western Washington University, like those supporting the Mastcam-Z instrument, provide the exact hands-on STEM training you champion through the American WINGS Act to prepare students for our local aerospace industry."

About Representative Rick Larsen (D-WA-02)

  • Has served as the United States representative for Washington's 2nd congressional district since 2001.
  • Holds a master's degree in public affairs from the University of Minnesota and previously worked as a lobbyist and director of public affairs for the Washington State Dental Association.
  • Has raised significant campaign funds from political action committees affiliated with the transportation and defense industries, including contributions from Boeing and ExxonMobil.
  • Formerly served on the House Armed Services Committee and has consistently voted for funding related to the wars in Iraq and Afghanistan.
  • Opposed the Green New Deal resolution in 2019, citing concerns regarding its potential impact on the necessity of air travel.
