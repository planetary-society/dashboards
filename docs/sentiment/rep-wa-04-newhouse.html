About Representative Dan Newhouse (R-WA-04)

  • Earned a Bachelor of Science degree in agricultural economics from Washington State University in 1977.
  • Served as the director of the Washington State Department of Agriculture from 2009 to 2013.
  • Operates a 600-acre farm in Sunnyside, Washington, producing hops, tree fruit, grapes, and alfalfa.
  • Advocated for U.S. leadership in biotechnology and alternative proteins, including cultivated meat, to ensure national food security.
  • Served four terms in the Washington House of Representatives prior to his election to the U.S. House of Representatives in 2014.
