About Representative Michael Baumgartner (R-WA-05)

  • Earned a degree in economics with minors in mathematics and French from Washington State University and a master's degree in public administration from Harvard University.
  • Served as an economics officer at the U.S. Embassy in Baghdad during the Iraq War and worked as a civilian contractor in Helmand Province, Afghanistan.
  • Previously served in the Washington State Senate from 2011 to 2019 and as the Spokane County treasurer from 2019 to 2024.
  • Co-taught a university course titled 'Leadership Lessons in Insurgent Warfare & Football Strategy' exploring strategy and tactics common in guerrilla warfare.
  • Led successful efforts to repeal a state law to establish a new medical school in Spokane and spearheaded a significant tuition reduction for public universities.
