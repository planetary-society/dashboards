About Representative Emily Randall (D-WA-06)

  • Elected to the U.S. House of Representatives in 2024 after serving in the Washington State Senate from 2019 to 2024.
  • The daughter of a shipyard worker, her district includes the Kitsap Peninsula and Naval Base Kitsap.
  • Introduced the bipartisan ANCHOR for Military Families Act to provide relocation resources, including school enrollment support and the Exceptional Family Member Program, for families during Permanent Change of Station moves.
  • Authored state legislation as a senator to allow children of service members to enroll in school before establishing a physical address in-district upon receiving transfer orders.
  • Professional background includes advocating for healthcare and education at Boston Children’s Hospital and the San Francisco AIDS Foundation.
