About Senator John Boozman (R-AR)

  • Graduated from the Southern College of Optometry in 1977 and co-founded a private regional eye clinic.
  • Served as a volunteer optometrist for low-income families and established a low vision program at the Arkansas School for the Blind.
  • Authored legislation focused on veteran employment and retraining, including the Veterans Retraining Act of 2009.
  • Maintains a background in agriculture, having raised Polled Hereford cattle and participated in the 4-H program.
  • Son of a U.S. Air Force Master Sergeant and has served as a member of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization Parliamentary Assembly (NATO PA).
