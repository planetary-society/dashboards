Meeting Strategy

Lead with: Lead with the Defender of Deep Space and Local Jobs

Lead by thanking the Senator for his vocal leadership in protecting the Mars Sample Return mission and the workforce at the Jet Propulsion Laboratory. You should frame the $9 billion funding request for the Science Mission Directorate as the necessary tool to prevent what the Senator calls 'national self-destruction' and to secure California's $2.9 billion annual share of NASA science obligations.

Bridge issues:

  • Economy and Working Families: "With NASA science obligations in California averaging $2.9 billion annually, supporting the $9 billion Science Mission Directorate budget is a direct investment in the middle-class, high-tech jobs you have fought to protect at JPL and across the state."

About Senator Adam B. Schiff (D-CA)

  • Served as a junior United States senator from California since 2024, following a 23-year tenure in the U.S. House of Representatives and four years in the California State Senate.
  • Earned a Bachelor of Arts in political science from Stanford University and a Juris Doctor from Harvard Law School.
  • Served as an assistant United States attorney from 1987 to 1993, notably prosecuting a former FBI agent for spying for the Soviet Union.
  • Chaired the House Intelligence Committee from 2019 to 2023 and served as a lead impeachment manager in the first impeachment trial of President Donald Trump.
  • Authored legislation to direct the USDA to develop a national strategy for protein diversification and support the development of alternative proteins, including cultivated meat.
