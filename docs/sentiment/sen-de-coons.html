About Senator Christopher A. Coons (D-DE)

  • Earned a Bachelor of Arts in chemistry and political science from Amherst College and later received a J.D. from Yale Law School and a master's degree in ethics from Yale Divinity School.
  • Spent eight years as in-house counsel for W.L. Gore & Associates, a manufacturer of high-tech materials and fabrics, where he managed federal government relations and the ethics training program.
  • Served as a volunteer relief worker in Kenya and worked for several nonprofit organizations, including the National Coalition for the Homeless and the 'I Have a Dream' Foundation.
  • Held local executive and legislative roles as the president of the New Castle County Council and as the New Castle County Executive prior to his Senate service.
  • Advocated for increased federal funding for carbon capture, use, and storage (CCUS) and sponsored the PROVE IT Act to collect data on the greenhouse gas intensity of manufactured goods.
