About Senator Raphael G. Warnock (D-GA)

  • Holds a Bachelor of Arts in psychology from Morehouse College, as well as a Master of Divinity, Master of Philosophy, and Doctor of Philosophy from Union Theological Seminary.
  • Serves as the senior pastor of Ebenezer Baptist Church in Atlanta and has authored multiple books on theology and public witness.
  • Advocated for green energy manufacturing in Georgia, including electric vehicles, batteries, and solar manufacturing, and hosted interfaith meetings on climate change.
  • Supported the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2022, securing funding for energy plant upgrades and barracks at Fort Stewart.
  • Introduced bipartisan legislation to prioritize the construction of Interstate 14 to support regional military installations and the economy.
