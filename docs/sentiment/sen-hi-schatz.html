About Senator Brian Schatz (D-HI)

  • Served in the Hawaii House of Representatives from 1998 to 2006 and as the 12th Lieutenant Governor of Hawaii from 2010 to 2012.
  • Held leadership roles in the nonprofit sector as CEO of Helping Hands Hawaii and director of the Center for a Sustainable Future.
  • Focuses on STEM-related policy areas including clean energy initiatives, cybersecurity, and the expansion of telehealth services.
  • Co-authored the NIST Small Business Cybersecurity Act and the Kids Off Social Media Act to regulate social media algorithms for minors.
  • Advocates for climate change legislation as a co-founder of the Senate Climate Change Task Force and negotiator for the Inflation Reduction Act of 2022.
