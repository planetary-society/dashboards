Meeting Strategy

Bridge issues:

  • Global Leadership & National Security: "By fully funding NASA science, we ensure the U.S. remains the global leader in space exploration, which directly reinforces your bipartisan efforts like the TASA Act to expand our strategic partnerships in the Indo-Pacific."

About Senator Tammy Duckworth (D-IL)

  • Served as a United States Army helicopter pilot during the Iraq War, where she became the first female double amputee of the conflict after her Black Hawk was hit by a rocket-propelled grenade.
  • Retired from the Illinois Army National Guard as a lieutenant colonel in 2014 after 23 years of military service.
  • Earned a Ph.D. in human services from Capella University with a dissertation focused on physicians' use of electronic medical records.
  • Served as Assistant Secretary for Public and Intergovernmental Affairs at the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs, where she led initiatives on veteran homelessness and digital accessibility.
  • Participated in the National Air and Space Museum's Military Women Aviators Oral History Initiative project in 2019.
