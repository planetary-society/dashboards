About Senator Roger Marshall (R-KS)

  • Served as a captain in the United States Army Reserve for seven years.
  • Holds a Bachelor of Science in biochemistry from Kansas State University and a Doctor of Medicine from the University of Kansas.
  • Practiced medicine as an obstetrician and gynecologist and served as chairman of the board of Great Bend Regional Hospital.
  • Served as vice president of Farmers Bank and Trust and was a district governor of Rotary International.
  • Previously served as the U.S. Representative for Kansas's 1st congressional district from 2017 to 2021 before his election to the U.S. Senate.
