About Senator Bill Cassidy (R-LA)

  • Practicing physician and gastroenterologist who earned a Doctor of Medicine from LSU School of Medicine and specialized in the treatment of liver disease.
  • Founded the Greater Baton Rouge Community Clinic to provide free healthcare to uninsured residents and led medical volunteers in converting an abandoned facility for hurricane victims.
  • Introduced the Energy Consumers Relief Act of 2013 and the Gulf Coast Jobs Preservation Act to address deep water drilling and EPA regulatory costs.
  • Co-sponsored the Foreign Pollution Fee Act, proposing a carbon tariff on energy and industrial imports based on emission intensity.
  • Advocated for medical and public health initiatives, including childhood vaccination programs and legislation to address the national obesity crisis.
