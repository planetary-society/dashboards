About Senator Tina Smith (D-MN)

  • Earned a Bachelor of Arts in political science from Stanford University and a Master of Business Administration from the Tuck School of Business at Dartmouth College.
  • Began her professional career in marketing at General Mills and later founded her own marketing firm consulting for businesses and nonprofits.
  • Served as the vice president of external affairs at Planned Parenthood of Minnesota, North Dakota, and South Dakota from 2003 to 2006.
  • Held executive leadership roles in Minnesota state government, serving as chief of staff to the Governor and later as the 48th Lieutenant Governor of Minnesota.
  • Gained early industry exposure working on the Trans-Alaska Pipeline in Prudhoe Bay, Alaska, prior to attending college.
