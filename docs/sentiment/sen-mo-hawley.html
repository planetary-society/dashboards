Bridge issues:

  • Be extremely careful not to oversell the Senator's past support for NASA science; his public record on the Science Mission Directorate is virtually non-existent and his recent focus has been on cutting federal research costs.

Expect this reaction: The office will likely be lukewarm and highly skeptical of funding levels that they perceive as subsidizing university overhead. About Senator Josh Hawley (R-MO)

  • Served as the 42nd Attorney General of Missouri from 2017 to 2019 before his election to the U.S. Senate.
  • Graduated from Stanford University with a degree in history and earned a Juris Doctor from Yale Law School.
  • Served as a law clerk for U.S. Supreme Court Chief Justice John Roberts and Judge Michael W. McConnell of the U.S. Court of Appeals for the Tenth Circuit.
  • Worked as an associate professor at the University of Missouri School of Law and as an attorney for the Becket Fund for Religious Liberty.
  • Authored several books, including 'Theodore Roosevelt, Preacher of Righteousness,' 'The Tyranny of Big Tech,' and 'Manhood: The Masculine Virtues America Needs.'
