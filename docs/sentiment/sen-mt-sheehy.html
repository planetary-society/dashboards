About Senator Tim Sheehy (R-MT)

  • Graduated from the U.S. Naval Academy with a bachelor's degree in history in 2008 and served as a Navy SEAL with tours in Iraq, Afghanistan, South America, and the Pacific.
  • Founded Bridger Aerospace in 2014, an aerial firefighting and wildfire management company that provides services across 24 states and two Canadian provinces.
  • Formed Ascent Vision Technologies (AVT), a company specializing in aerial surveillance, imaging systems, and technology for jamming and shooting down enemy drones.
  • Authored the 2023 memoir 'Mudslingers: A True Story of Aerial Firefighting' and was appointed to the United States Naval Academy's Board of Visitors in 2025.
  • Introduced and passed the 'Aerial Firefighting Enhancement Act of 2025,' which allows private companies to acquire former military aircraft for wildfire response.
