About Senator Thomas Tillis (R-NC)

  • Earned a Bachelor of Science in technology management from the University of Maryland University College in 1986.
  • Spent over two decades in the private sector, including roles at computer company Wang Laboratories and as a partner at the consulting firm Price Waterhouse.
  • Worked for IBM's consulting arm from 2002 until 2009, during which time he began his political career in local government.
  • Served as a member and Speaker of the North Carolina House of Representatives, where he oversaw the restructuring of the state's tax code and regulations.
  • Opposed a 10-year moratorium on state-level artificial intelligence regulation and has advocated for market-based climate solutions and offshore drilling.
