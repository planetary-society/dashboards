About Senator Deb Fischer (R-NE)

  • Served in the Nebraska Legislature from 2005 to 2013, where she chaired the Transportation and Telecommunications Committee and helped pass the BUILD Nebraska Act for infrastructure projects.
  • Earned a Bachelor of Science degree in education from the University of Nebraska–Lincoln in 1987.
  • Appointed by Governor Mike Johanns as a Commissioner to the Nebraska Coordinating Commission for Post-Secondary Education from 2000 to 2004.
  • Advocates for the modernization of the U.S. nuclear triad and increased defense spending to deter international adversaries including China, Iran, and Russia.
  • Maintains ties to the agricultural industry as a co-owner and operator of a family cattle ranch in Nebraska.
