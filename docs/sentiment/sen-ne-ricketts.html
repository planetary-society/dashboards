Meeting Strategy

Lead with: Strategic Security and Agricultural Utility

Lead by framing NASA science as a strategic asset for national security and Nebraska's agricultural economy. You should emphasize that maintaining leadership in space is essential for countering geopolitical competitors and protecting the satellite-derived data that Nebraska farmers rely on for precision agriculture and drought monitoring.

Bridge issues:

  • Boosting Nebraska Agriculture: "NASA’s Earth science missions provide the critical data our farmers in Nebraska need for drought monitoring and precision irrigation, ensuring our state's agricultural equipment remains the most efficient in the world."
  • Maintaining Peace Through Strength: "Consistent funding for the Science Mission Directorate is vital to winning the modern space race against China and ensuring that the United States, not our adversaries, defines the next generation of space technology."

Watch out for:

  • Avoid pitching NASA science as a purely academic or 'blank check' endeavor, as the Senator is a member of the Budget Committee and a vocal critic of what he terms 'reckless big government spending.'
  • Do not overstate his past support for specific science missions; while he voted for the broad NASA budget bill, he hasn't yet signed on to specific funding requests for the Science Mission Directorate.

Expect this reaction: The office is likely to be receptive to the importance of the mission but lukewarm regarding specific funding increases. Expect staff to emphasize fiscal restraint and the need to prioritize national security-aligned space projects over other scientific goals.

Key Challenges

  • Political Constraint: As a member of the Budget Committee, he consistently advocates for strict federal spending controls and may resist increases in discretionary funding.6 5

About Senator Pete Ricketts (R-NE)

  • Served as the 40th Governor of Nebraska from 2015 to 2023 before being appointed to the U.S. Senate in 2023.
  • Holds a Bachelor of Arts in biology and a Master of Business Administration in marketing and finance from the University of Chicago.
  • Extensive private sector experience as former Chief Operating Officer of TD Ameritrade and former employee of Union Pacific Railroad.
  • Co-founded and served as president of the Platte Institute for Economic Research and was a trustee of the American Enterprise Institute.
  • Maintains significant industry ties as a part owner of Major League Baseball's Chicago Cubs.
