About Senator Margaret Wood Hassan (D-NH)

  • Earned a B.A. in history from Brown University and a J.D. from the Northeastern University School of Law.
  • Served as associate general counsel for Brigham and Women’s Hospital and worked as an attorney for a corporate defense and business law firm.
  • Served two terms as the 81st governor of New Hampshire, during which she signed legislation creating a state sea level rise commission.
  • Appointed as a citizen advisor to the Advisory Committee to the Adequacy in Education and Finance Commission by the Governor of New Hampshire in 1999.
  • Daughter of Robert Coldwell Wood, a political scientist and former U.S. Secretary of Housing and Urban Development.
