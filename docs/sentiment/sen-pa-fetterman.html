About Senator John Fetterman (D-PA)

  • Served as the mayor of Braddock, Pennsylvania, from 2006 to 2019 and as the 34th lieutenant governor of Pennsylvania from 2019 to 2023.
  • Earned a bachelor's degree in finance from Albright College, an MBA from the University of Connecticut, and a Master of Public Policy from Harvard University.
  • Began a professional career in the insurance industry as a risk-management underwriter for Chubb before joining AmeriCorps to teach students pursuing GEDs.
  • Maintained a professional focus on community revitalization, youth programs, and criminal justice reform, including serving as chair of the Pennsylvania Board of Pardons.
  • Utilizes closed-captioning technology to assist with auditory processing and speech-related issues following an ischemic stroke in 2022.
