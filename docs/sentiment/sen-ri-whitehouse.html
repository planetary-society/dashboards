Lead with: NASA Science as the Gold Standard for Truth

Bridge issues:

  • Fighting the Climate Crisis: "Senator, you’ve frequently said you trust NASA scientists over industry lobbyists; we are asking for your support for the Science Mission Directorate to ensure those scientists have the funding to continue providing the authoritative climate data that Rhode Island’s coastal planning depends on."

About Senator Sheldon Whitehouse (D-RI)

  • Served as the United States Attorney for the District of Rhode Island (1993–1998) and as the 71st Attorney General of Rhode Island (1999–2003).
  • Holds a Juris Doctor from the University of Virginia School of Law and a bachelor's degree from Yale College.
  • Authored the Safeguarding America's Future and Environment (SAFE) Act and the U.S.-Israel Cybersecurity Cooperation Enhancement Act.
  • Maintains personal and professional ties to the maritime sector through his spouse, a marine biologist, and his legislative focus on ocean conservation and octopus farming bans.
  • Has delivered over 250 Senate floor speeches regarding climate change and has advocated for the use of the best available science in federal natural resource planning.
