About Senator Tim Scott (R-SC)

  • Worked in the financial services and insurance industries before entering politics, later owning the insurance agency Tim Scott Allstate.
  • Served in the U.S. House of Representatives from 2011 to 2013 and has served in the U.S. Senate since his appointment in 2013.
  • Graduated from Charleston Southern University in 1988 with a Bachelor of Science degree in political science.
  • Advocated for the cryptocurrency industry and supported strengthening the Committee on Foreign Investment in the United States to review foreign purchases of U.S. tech firms.
  • Maintains personal ties to the military through his older brother, who served as a sergeant major in the U.S. Army.
