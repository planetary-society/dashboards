About Senator John Thune (R-SD)

  • Holds a Bachelor of Arts in business from Biola University and a Master of Business Administration from the University of South Dakota.
  • Served as the Railroad Director of South Dakota from 1991 to 1993 and as executive director of the South Dakota Municipal League from 1993 to 1996.
  • Advocated for 5G deployment and rural broadband expansion, authoring legislation to address telecommunications infrastructure and workforce challenges.
  • Co-authored the TRACED Act to increase federal authority and mandate authentication technology to combat illegal robocalls.
  • Requested briefings on the security of the U.S. hardware supply chain following reports of foreign tampering with server motherboards.
