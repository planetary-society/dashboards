About Senator Bill Hagerty (R-TN)

  • Served as the 30th United States Ambassador to Japan from 2017 to 2019 and was previously an economic advisor and White House Fellow under President George H.W. Bush.
  • Extensive private sector experience in private equity, including co-founding Hagerty Peterson & Company and serving as a managing director at Trident Capital in Silicon Valley.
  • Served as Commissioner of the Tennessee Department of Economic and Community Development, overseeing investments from major industrial firms such as Nissan, Bridgestone, and Magneti Marelli.
  • Held board positions for several corporations, including CyMed, Houghton Mifflin Harcourt, Pinnacle Financial Partners, and R.J. O'Brien.
  • Academic background includes a Bachelor of Arts in economics and business administration and a Juris Doctor, both from Vanderbilt University.
