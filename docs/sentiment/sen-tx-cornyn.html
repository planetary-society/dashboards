Meeting Strategy

Lead with: Science as the Vanguard of the Space Race

Since the Senator views space through the lens of national security and geopolitical competition with China, you should frame robotic science as the essential precursor to the human missions he champions. Emphasize that NASA's Science Mission Directorate provides the 'eyes and ears' that identify resources and ensure crew safety, securing the American technological edge. Given the thin direct SMD record, treat this as the best available opening rather than evidence of likely support.

Bridge issues:

  • Foreign Affairs and National Security: "By supporting NASA's planetary science missions, we ensure that American technology remains the global standard, preventing adversaries like China from dominating the strategic 'high ground' of scientific discovery in our solar system."
  • Local Economic Impact: "NASA science is a massive economic engine for Texas, with over $3 billion in obligations recently supporting world-class research at institutions like the Southwest Research Institute and Rice University."

Watch out for:

  • Do not assume the Senator is an automatic supporter of science funding; his record on the Science Mission Directorate is thin, and he has a history of voting against NASA funding in the name of fiscal restraint.
  • Do not oversell science-specific support when the public record is thin; keep the ask anchored in local value and broad NASA space science.

Expect this reaction: The office will likely be enthusiastic about NASA's importance to Texas but non-committal or lukewarm on specific funding increases for science programs, especially if they are not directly tied to human spaceflight or national security.

About Senator John Cornyn (R-TX)

  • Son of a U.S. Air Force colonel who attended the American School in Japan before graduating from Trinity University and earning law degrees from St. Mary's University and the University of Virginia.
  • Served as a district judge in San Antonio and as an associate justice on the Texas Supreme Court before being elected as the Attorney General of Texas.
  • Established the Texas Internet Bureau in 2000 to investigate cybercrime, including hacker break-ins, consumer fraud, and online child exploitation.
  • Advocated for alternatives to human embryonic stem cell research, supporting federal funding for research utilizing adult and cord blood stem cells instead.
  • Introduced the Ukraine Democracy Defense Lend-Lease Act of 2022 and has supported the Special Immigrant Visa program for Afghan interpreters who assisted U.S. forces.
