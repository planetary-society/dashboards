About Senator James C. Justice (R-WV)

  • Holds a Bachelor's degree and a Master of Business Administration from Marshall University.
  • Inherited and managed a vast family business empire including Bluestone Coal Corporation and Bluestone Industries, which at one point included 70 active mines across five states.
  • Founded Bluestone Farms in 1977, growing it into a 50,000-acre operation that became the leading grain producer on the U.S. East Coast.
  • Served two terms as the 36th Governor of West Virginia from 2017 to 2025 before his election to the U.S. Senate.
  • Maintains a long-term involvement in youth sports, serving as president of Beckley Little League since 1992 and coaching high school girls' basketball since 2003.
